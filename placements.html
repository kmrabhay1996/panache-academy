THE PANACHE PLACEMENT SYSTEM

Four steps. One job offer.

  1. 01

    Skill mastery

    Six months of industry-grade training. Aviation regulations, in-flight service, grooming protocols, hospitality operations — taught by ex-cabin crew, hotel managers and travel veterans.

  2. 02

    Interview prep

    Resume workshops, group discussions, mock interviews recorded on camera, body-language coaching, accent-neutral spoken English. Until you can walk into any panel.

  3. 03

    On-campus drives

    Recruiters from Emirates, Qatar, IndiGo, Vistara, Taj, Oberoi and Marriott visit our campuses for direct hiring drives. We bring the jobs to you.

  4. 04

    Continued mentorship

    Joined Emirates? Got assigned to a tough Doha route? Your Panache mentor is on WhatsApp. We support every alum into their first job and beyond.
