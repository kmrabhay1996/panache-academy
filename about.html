THE FOUNDER

A career built in the air — taught from the heart.

"I built Panache because the industry I came from didn't have a school that taught the human side of it."

Khushnum Avari spent her career in the aviation industry — first as an air hostess, later as an instructor and corporate trainer. She founded Panache Academy with one simple intent: create a learning environment where the practical realities of aviation, hospitality and tourism are taught by people who have actually lived them.

What started as a single campus in Ahmedabad now spans six cities across India — Ahmedabad (Shyamal & Nikol), Vadodara, Indore, Goa and Udaipur. The classrooms have grown. The promise has not changed.

MILESTONES

How Panache grew, chapter by chapter.

Six campuses. Three accreditations. Twenty-three named industry partners. Here's how it came together.

  1. Chapter 01

    The vision

    Khushnum Avari leaves her career as an airhostess, instructor and corporate trainer to build the academy she wishes had existed when she first walked into an aviation interview.

  2. Chapter 02

    First campus, Ahmedabad

    Panache opens its doors at Shyamal Cross Roads in Ahmedabad — a single floor of classrooms with one promise: industry-grade training at a price that respects the families who pay for it.

  3. Chapter 03

    Industry partnerships

    Direct placement relationships established with Emirates, Qatar Airways, Etihad, IndiGo, Vistara, Taj, Oberoi, Marriott, Cox & Kings and other industry leaders. Recruiters begin visiting the campus.

  4. Chapter 04

    Quality accreditation

    Panache earns ISO 9001:2015 certification for its quality management systems, Grade 'A' from the Confederation of International Accreditation Commission, and formal accreditation from the Knowledge Consortium of Gujarat.

  5. Chapter 05

    Network expansion

    Second Ahmedabad campus opens in Nikol. New campuses follow in Vadodara, Indore, Goa and Udaipur — one curriculum, one standard, five cities, six campuses.

  6. Today

    Where Panache stands now

    Thirteen programs across Certificate, Diploma, BBA, MBA and specialized tracks. Forty-six certified faculty. Twenty-three named industry partners. Alumni in Emirates, Qatar Airways, IndiGo, Taj and beyond. The classrooms have grown. The promise hasn't changed.
