OUR STORY

Style with substance — taught by people who've lived it.

Panaché is a word that amalgamates style with substance. It captures what every student carries with them when they leave our academy — confidence built on real skill.

Founded by Khushnum Avari, an ex-airhostess and corporate trainer, Panache has spent over a decade turning ambitious students into professionals at Emirates, Qatar Airways, IndiGo, Taj, Oberoi and beyond. We don't just teach the syllabus — we mentor the human.
